Read Profile of Oakheights College Sagamu

Oakheights College Sagamu (OCS) is a government approved,private and co-educational institution that accomodates both boarding and day students. OCS was founded on 25th September, 2006 by Engr. Ibraheem Fayemi. The school is located at Igbepa road Agbele Sagamu. Oakheights College Sagamu is the first choice of students who seek a dynamic,innovative, and student-centered secondary education,the environment is very conducive for effective learning. OCS affirms that academic success is linked to the excellence of our residential life, and we commit ourselves to pursuing that excellence in order to enhance the learning environment at the institution. OCS strives to create an environment that cherishes both the individual and provides a strong sense of community, Students are able to learn to make personal decisions regarding time to care for their own physical and emotional well-being. Management has kept the standard of education in the school by ensuring that all teachers possess a minimum of a Bachelor's degree in their teaching subject. The school encourages sport activities and the various teams have been competing with other schools in some sporting events such as football and other track event. The school has a soccer field with plans to construct a sports complex to foster team spirit and healthy competition amongst the students, the school celebrates an annual inter-house sports competition. Apart from academic excellence of its students, OCS is also known for its high moral standard and emphasise upon character building, students are taught in a disciplined environment, inculcating in them the attributes of good behaviour, honesty, hardwork, respect for authority and consideration for others.
